Choosing the beans and roast options.

Coffee beans come in a light, medium or dark roast. Usually lighter roasts contain more caffeine and have a brighter taste.

Filters

There are many ways to make a pour-over cup of coffee, some require a paper filter and others don't. Each coffee bean is unique and may require a new recipe.

Pouring and brewing

Grind the beans and place in the filter. Boil the water in a kettle and pour over the ground beans in a clockwise pattern. Wait twenty seconds between each pour and allow enough water to filter through between each pour. Serve and enjoy!

Better Extraction

Pour-over coffee is the best way to extract coffee from the beans.

Exceptional Taste

This method of brewing coffee results in the best flavor.

Efficient

From start to finish, grinding and brewing the coffee should take less than five minutes.
